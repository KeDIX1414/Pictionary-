Let's Play Pictionary

Hello and welcome to our game of Pictionary! Make sure to read the rules below. Press the start button when you are ready to play!

  1. Press the play button when you are ready to begin. You will be alerted when enough players are ready, and the game is beginning.
  2. Like a normal game of Pictionary, you will take turns drawing and guessing. When it is your turn to draw, you will be told the object you have to draw and the time remaing.
  3. When it is your turn to guess, write your answer in the textbox when you are ready.

Thank you for your patience. The game will begin shortly. Do not refresh the page.
